Books

It's not just tv shows and movies that are the only types of entertainment, it's books! I read in my free time and consider that to be very productive and at times, entertaining. I believe books make you grow more than you think. Most people would likely not read but if you do something you don't like you can end up tolerating it and eventually liking it, it's called putting yourself in an uncomfortable zone.
